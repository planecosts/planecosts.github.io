PlaneCosts

This tool is designed to help you decide whether you should continue to rent, or buy your own plane by walking you through the many associated costs of ownership.

Any costs estimates shown here should be used strictly as guides, and not taken as financial advice.

Current Costs

Fixed Costs
How much are your loan payments per month?

What are your anticipated annual insurance costs?
(avg is $1200/yr)

What are your anticipated annual inspection costs?
(avg is $1800/yr)

What are your anticipated monthly storage costs?
(avg tie-down: $100/mo, avg t-hangar: $350/mo)

How much do you intend to put aside for incidental maintenance?
(Most owners estimate $1000-2000/yr)

Operating Costs
(Cost per hour to run your new plane)

What is the average cost per gallon of fuel in your area?

How much (in dollars) oil do you estimate you new plane burns per hour?
($2/hr is a sufficient estimate for most single engine aircraft)

How much per tach hour do you plan on contributing to your engine overhaul fund?
(at an avg overhaul cost of $20000 every 2000hrs, a minimum of $10/hr is recommended)

How much per tach hour do you plan on contributing to your propellor overhaul fund?
(at an avg overhaul cost of $5000 every 2000hrs, a minimum of $2.50/hr is recommended)
